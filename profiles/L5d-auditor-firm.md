## Identity + context

External auditor at mid-size accounting firm (Big-4 or large-regional). Engagement-based work: multiple concurrent audit engagements, each with team (partner + senior + staff). Produces accountability-bearing audit reports + working papers + opinions under regulatory scrutiny (PCAOB, SEC, state boards).

Multi-user multi-team workspace: per-engagement teams; shared firm-wide policies; engagement-level confidentiality boundaries; partner-level signatures; cross-engagement quality reviews.

Adds variation to L5b/L5c — engagement-based work model; partner authority hierarchy; cyclical regulatory environment; high-volume claim production per engagement.

## What this profile is intended to EXEMPLIFY

This profile stress-tests:
- **Engagement-based workspace partition**: multiple engagements concurrent; per-engagement boundary; cross-engagement firm-wide policies
- **Hierarchical authority**: partner signs; senior reviews; staff drafts. Per-claim authority attribution.
- **Regulatory cyclical work**: annual audit cycle; consistent workflow patterns year-over-year
- **High-volume claim production**: hundreds-thousands of claims per engagement; per-claim audit-trail at scale
- **Cross-engagement confidentiality**: information barriers between concurrent engagements (e.g., competing client engagements)
- **Cross-engagement quality reviews**: firm's QC partner reviews multiple engagements; meta-audit of audits
- **Document-heavy artifact production**: working papers; testing documentation; report sections; per-section attribution
- **Regulatory inspection defensibility**: PCAOB inspection examines documentation; six-months-later test concrete and frequent
- **Multi-tenant + multi-engagement** governance complexity (overlap with L4b firm IT)
