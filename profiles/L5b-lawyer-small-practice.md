## Identity + context

Practicing attorney at solo practice or small firm. Domain: typical legal practice areas (litigation, transactional, family, immigration, etc.). Produces accountability-bearing legal documents (briefs, motions, contracts, opinions) under professional responsibility + court rules + bar discipline. Defensibility under opposing-counsel scrutiny + court review + bar oversight.

Hypothetical-but-realistic profile (no specific lawyer; constructed from typical legal-practice patterns). Adds variation to L5a planner — different regulatory shape (court rules + bar discipline vs administrative law + Stellungnahme); different defensibility scenario (opposing counsel + judge vs UNB + Bundesplanungsgesetz challenge).

## What this profile is intended to EXEMPLIFY

This profile stress-tests:
- **Legal-practice defensibility**: court + opposing counsel + bar discipline as defensibility scenario
- **Citation-heavy work**: per-claim citation tracking; case-law citation; statutory citation; verification under cross-examination
- **Adversarial-context defensibility**: opposing counsel actively challenges; defensibility test sharpened
- **Time-pressure dynamics**: court filing deadlines; statute of limitations; emergency motions
- **Multi-matter parallelism**: typical lawyer has many matters concurrent; multiple work-units in workspace
- **Conflict-of-interest concerns**: cross-matter knowledge boundaries; ethical walls
- **Privilege concerns**: attorney-client privilege; work-product doctrine; framework-level privacy concerns
- **Cross-jurisdictional realities**: even "solo lawyer" often crosses jurisdictions (federal + state; multi-state for some practices)
